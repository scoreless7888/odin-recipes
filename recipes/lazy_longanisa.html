Lazy Longanisa

This is a varation of a popular Filipino breakfast referred to as “silog”. There are other varieties that vary the meat and/or pork used with the dish. Typically, garlic fried rice and over easy eggs accompany the choice of meat. Common meats can be (but are not limited to) tapa, longanisa, bangus, bacon, and spam.

The choice of rice and style of eggs can also vary depending on individual preference. Freshly cooked rice can be prepared, or a variation of fried rice can be used instead. Some may also prefer to have their eggs more thoroughly cooked.

Regardless, there is no wrong way to prepare a Filipino- favorite dish. Its flexibility is what makes it popular among Filipinos.
